1. kubectl get pods (To get pods)
2. kubectl get pods -n <namespace> (To get pods in a specific namespace)
3. kubectl describe pod <pod-name> (Gives detailed info of a pod)
4. kubectl describe pod <pod-name> -n <namespace> (Gives detailed info of a pod in a specific namespace)
5. kubectl get pod my-pod -o yaml (Gives yaml of a pod)
6. kubectl get pods -o wide (Gives detailed info of a pod)
